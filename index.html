FAQ

Common Questions

Are your land properties legally documented? +

Yes. Every plot listed by WealthSpring Properties comes with verified documentation including Right of Occupancy (R of O) or Certificate of Occupancy (C of O), Survey Plans, and Government Allocation letters where applicable. We do not list undocumented land.

Where in Abuja do you currently operate? +

We currently have active listings in Gwarinpa, Kuje, Lugbe, and other growing districts within the FCT. We are actively expanding and will soon have listings in other major Nigerian cities.

View Listings
Can I visit the site before making a purchase? +

Absolutely, and we strongly encourage it. You can schedule a free site inspection by contacting us through the website or on WhatsApp. Our team will accompany you to the property.

What payment plans are available? +

We offer flexible payment options including outright payment with discounts and installment plans spread across agreed timelines. Contact us directly for a payment plan tailored to your situation.

How does the buying process work? +

The process is straightforward: browse and select a plot, schedule an inspection, agree on price and payment terms, make payment, then receive your allocation letter and full documentation. Our team guides you at every step.

What is SpringAI and how does it help? +

SpringAI is WealthSpring's AI-powered chat assistant. It answers questions about our properties, pricing, locations, and the buying process at any time of day or night. For anything it does not have clear information about, it transfers you directly to our team on WhatsApp.
